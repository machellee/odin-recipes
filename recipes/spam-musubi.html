Spam Musubi

Description

This recipe will teach you how to make a popular dish from Hawaii. Spam musubi is simple and easy dish made from common ingredients in a Hawaiian household.

While spam may not be so often used here in the mainland it is a popular ingredient used in many places in the pacific islands like the Phillipines.

Ingredients

  • Spam
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Soy sauce
  • Sugar
  • Nori
  • Sushi Rice

Steps

  1. Slice the spam into 8-10 pieces and put in ziploc bag
  2. Mix oyster sauce, soy sauce, and sugar until sugar is dissolved and add into the bag
  3. Drain off the marinade and fry the spam
  4. Place a strip of nori on cutting board and put musubi mold on the middle of the nori
  5. Add sushi rice into the mold and press down
  6. Remove mold and place cooked spam ontop of the rice
  7. Wrap the nori up from both sides and serve
